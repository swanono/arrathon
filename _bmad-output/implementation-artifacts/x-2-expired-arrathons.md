# Story X.2: Expired Arrathons Behaviour

Status: brainstorm

## Open Questions

- Do we show past arrathons in the home list? Hidden, grayed out, or separate section?
- Should an expired arrathon be read-only (no join, no invite)?
- Do we send a notification/summary after an arrathon ends?
- What defines "expired" — date only, or a manual "close" action by the organiser?
- Should the invite link be deactivated after the date?

## Options

**A** — Filter out past arrathons from home list (clean, but lose history)
**B** — Badge "Passé" + grayed card, still accessible
**C** — Two sections in home: "À venir" / "Passés" (best UX long-term)
**D** — Organiser manually closes the arrathon (explicit lifecycle)
